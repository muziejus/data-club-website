The “JAM” in “Jamstack” stands for “JavaScript, APIs, and Markdown,” and it
is an approach to building websites that contrasts with the more
traditional “LAMP” structure, or “Linux, Apache, MySQL, PHP.” 

No prior programming experience is required.
