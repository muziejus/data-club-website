Working with unstructured, textual data in Python presents new challenges.
We can use some of our familiar pandas idioms to organize our corpus of
text documents, but even a surface knowledge of the corpus demands new
tools for analyzing data. Here, we’ll build a corpus of text and begin
looking for macro trends in it.
